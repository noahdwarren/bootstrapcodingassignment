About Me

I am a long time dnd player turned dm. I have played dnd for 6 years and have been a dm for the past 4 years.

I became a dm because I grew tired of how limiting the base game is and felt that I could improve upon it with new homebrew spells, abilites, classes, and much more.

About the Game

  • My game will take place on Roll20.
  • We will be using Discord for the voice chat function and general information.
  • My game is a heavily homebrewed world with homebrew rules, if that is not something that interests you than I can't recomend playing in this game.
  • My game is narative based, therefor I expect you to interact with the world through your character, now I will be doing my best to engage you in the world but that is a two way street.
  • My game goes to at least level 20 and sometimes to level 25, so with that in mind, late game will get mechanically complex.
  • My goal is to make sure you are having fun, but I will not sacrifice everyone else's fun for the sake of one person as this is a team game.
